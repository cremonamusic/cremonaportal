Terms & Conditions

Cremona Music of Strings Terms and Conditions

Payment of Fees

  • All school fees are to be paid before the 7th of the month, failing which a penalty charge of RM20 will be levied after the 8th of the month. No reminder letters or invoices will be given. The school reserves the right to suspend or terminate the students' lessons until all outstanding fees are paid.
  • Lesson fees can be paid through Bank Transfer (CREMONA MUSIC OF STRINGS, Ambank, 245-2022003-182). Fees made through bank in and online transfers have to screenshot payment slip to 012-2988255 or Email: cremonastrings@gmail.com.
  • New students upon enrolment pay a non-refundable registration fee of RM150 as well as one month advance fee.
  • Fees are payable throughout the year, inclusive of school vacation periods and public holidays. If the teacher is absent, the teacher will arrange replacement lessons. Alternatively, the school will arrange for a relief teacher to take over the class.
  • To terminate lessons, one month (calendar month) advance written notice must be given. Upon notification of termination, the remaining 4 weeks' lesson will be made good subject to the teacher and the school justification. All lessons are to be consumed within the period of 4 consecutive weeks from the official date of termination. No additional replacement will be made if a lesson falls on public or school holidays.
  • Students who absent themselves for any period of the time must continue to pay fees. Otherwise it is considered a termination.
  • The school reserves all rights to amend its fee periodically.

Attendance

  • Students are responsible for regular attendance. Students who are late for a lesson cannot be guaranteed their full lesson time.
  • If lessons must be cancelled, parents may notify through mobile phone (012-2988255). Please give at least two days notice when cancelling a lesson. No rescheduling of lessons if given notification on its day.
  • Lessons missed due to medical conditions can be rescheduled if given a valid medical certificate.
  • Lessons change not more than 6 times a year.
  • Replacement lessons can only be rescheduled once.
  • Failure to notify absence of lessons are declared forfeit.

Trial Lesson Terms & Conditions

  1. The purpose of a trial lesson is to allow the teacher to assess the readiness and suitability of the student for the particular course.
  2. It is not a guarantee that the trial student's enrolment for the course is confirmed. There is a possibility that the teacher may decide that the student is not ready or suited to pick up the instrument at that particular point in time. As such, the trial fee paid for the trial lesson is non-refundable and will not be used to offset for a course taken at a later stage in time.
  3. Confirmation of the trial lesson is upon payment of trial fee, at least one day before the trial lesson date.
  4. Trial fee can only be offset as one regular lesson fee if the student signs up on the same day of the trial lesson, for the same course type (e.g. instrument, individual/group, length of duration), under the same teacher (pending teacher's assessment). Thus, the course is considered to have commenced with immediate effect. There should be no postponement on the date of commencement of the course. Otherwise, it is considered as one trial lesson and is non-transferable and non-refundable.
  5. All Trial Lessons are not given priority over regular lessons even if the trial fee has been paid. If there is no vacancy left in a group class, the trial student will be scheduled onto another time slot, depending on availability. Otherwise, the trial fee will be refunded upon request.

General

  • Lesson fees are payable through Bank Transfer or TnG e-wallet.
  • Cremona Music reserves the right to take pictures of students for publicity and promotional purposes.
  • Cremona Music reserves the right to include, delete or change any of the above terms and conditions with or without prior notice.
