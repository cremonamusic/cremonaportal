Account Deletion

Delete Your Cremona Music Account

Cremona Music Portal & mobile app — CREMONA CO. LTD · 계정 삭제 안내

Delete your account from the app or website

  1. Sign in to the Cremona Music mobile app or the portal at portal.cremonamusic.com.
  2. Open Account (in the app: the Account tab; on the website: your account page).
  3. Choose Delete My Account and confirm.

Your account is deactivated immediately and you are signed out of all devices.

Request deletion by email

If you can no longer sign in, email op@cremonamusic.com from the email address linked to your Cremona Music account with the subject "Delete my account". We will process the request within 7 days.

What is deleted, and what is kept

  • Deleted: your login credentials, profile information and photo, and your access to messages, schedules, and reports.
  • Kept temporarily: academy records that involve other people — such as progress reports written by teachers, payment and tuition records, and messages already delivered to other users — are retained for up to 90 days (or longer where Korean law requires, e.g. financial records), then permanently removed.

You can also ask us to delete specific data (for example a profile photo or a conversation) without deleting your whole account — contact op@cremonamusic.com or use the in-app Support chat.
