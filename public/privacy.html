Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Cremona Music

1. Introduction

Cremona Music ("Cremona Music", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who access the Cremona Music Portal ("Portal"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using this Portal, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to all authorised users of the Cremona Music Portal — current employees, teachers, and contractors of Cremona Music who have been granted access to the Portal.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or submit personal information to use this Portal. However, if you contact us via email (info@cremonamusic.com), we will receive your name and email address as part of that communication.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access the Portal, our server or hosting provider may automatically log:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages accessed and time of access
  • Referring URL

This information is collected for security monitoring, troubleshooting, and usage analytics only.

3.3 Third-Party Services

This Portal links to third-party services (Google Drive, Google Classroom, Google Meet, EBricks, NE Times, etc.). When you click through to these services, those providers may collect data about you independently. Cremona Music has no control over and accepts no responsibility for the data practices of third-party services. Please review their respective privacy policies.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To maintain the security and integrity of the Portal
  • To monitor for unauthorised access or misuse
  • To troubleshoot technical issues
  • To respond to enquiries or support requests sent to info@cremonamusic.com
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to the operation of this Portal.

5. How We Store & Protect Your Information

Access logs and any data collected are stored on secured servers. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Access to log data is restricted to authorised Cremona Music management personnel only.

We retain access logs for a maximum of 90 days, after which they are deleted unless required for an ongoing security investigation or legal matter.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party except:

  • Service providers who assist in operating the Portal, under strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal authorities, if required by applicable law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Cremona Music or its staff.

7. Cookies

This Portal does not use cookies directly. However, third-party services you access through the Portal (e.g. Google) may place cookies on your device. Please refer to those providers' cookie policies for details.

8. Your Rights

As a data subject under applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data, where we are not legally required to retain it.
  • Objection — object to processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@cremonamusic.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

This Portal is intended for adult teaching professionals only. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has accessed the Portal inappropriately, please notify us immediately at info@cremonamusic.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any revisions. Continued use of the Portal after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes, we will notify users via email where possible.

11. Contact

For any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy:

  • Email: info@cremonamusic.com
