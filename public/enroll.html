Terms & Conditions

Payment

  • Tuition fees are payable monthly, in advance, on or before the 1st of each month.
  • A one-time registration fee is payable on enrolment and is non-refundable.
  • Fees remain payable in full for months containing public holidays; the annual lesson count is fixed at the start of each term.
  • Late payment beyond 14 days may result in lessons being suspended until the account is settled.
  • Materials, examination entry fees and accompanist fees are billed separately.

Attendance

  • Please arrive five minutes before the lesson start time. Lessons end at the scheduled time regardless of late arrival.
  • Absence must be notified at least 24 hours in advance. Lessons cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice are forfeited.
  • A maximum of two replacement lessons per term may be arranged, subject to teacher availability.
  • Lessons cancelled by the academy or the teacher will always be replaced or credited in full.
  • Persistent absence without notice may result in the lesson slot being reassigned.

Trial Lesson

  • The trial lesson is charged at the standard single-lesson rate and is payable at booking.
  • Trial lessons may be rescheduled once, with at least 24 hours' notice.
  • Should the student continue after the trial, the trial fee is credited towards the first month's tuition.
  • The academy reserves the right to recommend a different instrument, level or teacher following the trial.

General

  • One calendar month's written notice is required to withdraw from lessons.
  • The academy may photograph or record performances for promotional use; please notify us in writing to opt out.
  • Parents are responsible for their children before and after lesson times on the premises.
